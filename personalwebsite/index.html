Research

The Influence of Fiber Alignment on Cell Migration Patterns and Morphology

Summer 2024

Overview

Led computational modeling research, utilizing 20μm × 20μm grid networks to study cell-substrate interactions. Analyzed cell migration across multiple fiber configurations, measuring displacements up to 150μm and orientation distributions across 360° to map movement patterns.

Key Finding

  • Identified substrate stiffness variations of 5–20 kPa significantly impacting cell migration behavior and displacement trajectories in fiber networks.
